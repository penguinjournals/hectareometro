How big are 300 hectares?

300 hectares equal 3,000,000 square metres (3.00 km²) — about 420 football fields or 741 acres. But a figure like that is hard to picture: drag the map above to your city or a place you know to see these 300 hectares drawn at real scale.

To picture it, 300 hectares cover roughly the same as:

  • New York's Central Park (~341 ha)
  • twice London's Hyde Park (142 ha)
  • one and a half times the Principality of Monaco (202 ha)
