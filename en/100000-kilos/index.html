How heavy is 100,000 kilos?

The drawing above shows 100,000 kilos as 16.7 elephants: each icon represents 1 African elephant (6,000 kg). It is roughly the weight of 1,300 people.

In other units, 100,000 kilos is 220,462 pounds or 100 tonnes. Change the number in the tool or pick another reference for the drawing (eggs, people, cows, cars, elephants…) with the "Show" selector.
