How big are 100 hectares?

100 hectares equal 1,000,000 square metres (1.00 km²) — about 140 football fields or 247 acres. But a figure like that is hard to picture: drag the map above to your city or a place you know to see these 100 hectares drawn at real scale.

To picture it, 100 hectares cover roughly the same as:

  • London's Hyde Park (~142 ha)
  • half of the Principality of Monaco (the whole country is 202 ha)
  • more than twice the grounds of Vatican City (44 ha)
